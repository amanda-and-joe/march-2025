Travel

  • We recommend flying into Jacksonville International Airport (JAX)
  • The airport is ~40 min from the Beaches area (Ubers are available)
  • The Beaches area is walkable, so a car is not needed unless you want one
  • If you do want to explore, Amelia Island and St. Augustine are good day trip options less than 1 hour away

Recommendations

Our favorite spots to eat and drink in town:

Breakfast & Coffee

  • Homespun Kitchen - try the Acai Punch smoothie
  • Le Petit Paris Cafe - foofy drinks and sandwiches
  • Southern Grounds - cafe with nice outdoor patio seating

Lunch & Dinner

  • TacoLu - favorite stop for tacos
  • Reve Brewing + Bones Pizza - local brewery serving wood fired pizzas

Sweets

  • Whit's Frozen Custard - Amanda worked here in high school
  • Peterbrooke Chocolatier - home of the famous McCawley chocolate popcorn

Drinks and Hang Out

  • Lemon Bar - outdoor beachside bar with fun drinks
  • Pete's Bar - popular local watering hole
